Our team talked about our possible ways of communications and decided to choose Artificial Intelligence (AI) as the core of our topic.

We presented our topic and was asked to make the topic more focused instead of having a lot of subtopics. As a group, we decided to make our topic focus on planetary exploration and continued with our brainstorming to improve the outcome of our project.
